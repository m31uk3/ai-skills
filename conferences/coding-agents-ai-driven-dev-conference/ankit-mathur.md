## Background

Ankit Mathur is a software engineer with six years of experience focused on production ML infrastructure and model serving. He most recently served as Engineering Lead for AI Serving at Databricks, where he focused on systems problems in LLM serving and scaling generative AI for real-world, massive datasets. He has since departed Databricks and joined Abridge to lead engineering for AI Clinical Decision Support.

## Key Contributions

- **Databricks Model Serving**: Led the engineering team responsible for Databricks' model serving platform, tackling core systems challenges in LLM serving at enterprise scale.
- **Dolly LLM**: Co-author of Databricks' Dolly, listed alongside Ali Ghodsi, Matei Zaharia, and Reynold Xin on the paper "Free Dolly: Introducing the World's First Truly Open Instruction-Tuned LLM" (2023).
- **Batch Inference**: Co-authored "Demystifying Batch Inference On Databricks" covering how Databricks Serverless Batch Inference simplifies GenAI workloads at scale.
- **MLflow contributions**: Active open-source contributor to MLflow, improving Pyfunc save/load/log flows for PyTorch and Keras with custom-object support and GPU-aware behavior.
