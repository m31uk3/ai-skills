## Background

Erin Ahmed is the Head of Product at Cleric, an AI startup building autonomous systems for on-call engineering support. She joined Cleric in January 2025. Before Cleric, Erin spent three years as a Sr. Principal Product Manager at Slalom (January 2022 - January 2025), where she was recognized as a consulting leader. She also brings experience from prior roles at Driver.

- **Cross-functional leadership**: Known for bridging technical and business domains, with expertise in technical product management, systems thinking, and lean software development.

## About Cleric

Cleric is an AI teammate that frees software engineers from on-call support burden. Rather than simply summarizing alerts, Cleric reasons across alerts, logs, and dependencies to surface a single root cause instead of 15 symptoms. It learns like a senior engineer would and shares that knowledge across the team. The company was founded by Shahram Anver (CEO).
