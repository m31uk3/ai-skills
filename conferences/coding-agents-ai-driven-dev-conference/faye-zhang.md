## Background

Faye Zhang is a staff AI engineer and tech lead at Pinterest, where she leads Multimodal AI work for search traffic discovery, driving significant user growth globally. She combines expertise in large-scale distributed systems with cutting-edge NLP and AI agent research from her ongoing graduate studies at Stanford.

Prior to her current role at Pinterest, Faye held positions at EasyPost (where she developed a SmartRate API optimizing shipping rates, processing millions of requests daily), Reputation, and the Georgia Tech Research Institute.
