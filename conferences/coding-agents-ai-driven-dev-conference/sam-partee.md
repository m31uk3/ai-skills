## Background

Sam Partee is a pioneering engineer in AI and the co-founder and CTO of Arcade.dev. Before founding Arcade, Sam served as Principal AI Engineer at Redis, where he helped architect over 100 LLM-based applications for customers and led the AI engineering team that turned Redis's vector database into their hottest product line. He has deep roots in high-performance computing, having created SmartSim at Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) to connect the world's fastest supercomputers with ML infrastructure. He is also lead author on research applying AI to climate science.

## Key Contributions

- **Arcade.dev**: Co-founded the company to solve the fundamental problem of AI agent authentication — enabling agents to interact with enterprise and consumer services (Gmail, Slack, GitHub, Salesforce) securely using the credentials of the humans they assist.
- **Open-source**: Major contributor to LangChain, LlamaIndex, Chapel, and DeterminedAI.
- **Redis AI**: Architected 100+ LLM-based applications and led the team that made Redis's vector database a breakout product.
- **SmartSim (HPE)**: Created infrastructure connecting supercomputers with ML pipelines.
- **Climate AI research**: Lead author on applying AI to climate science problems.

## Arcade.dev — Key Details

- **Product**: An MCP runtime that enables AI agents to connect to integrations and act on behalf of users through authenticated tools. Solves the agent-to-tool authentication problem.
- **Funding**: Emerged from stealth with $12 million in funding.
- **Co-founder**: Alex Salazar, who teamed up with Sam in 2024 with a shared mission to fix what's broken in AI agents by giving them proper access and tool integration.
